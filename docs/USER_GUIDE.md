# User guide

This guide explains how to use the Kanban board. No technical knowledge is required.

---

## Getting started

### Accessing the app

Open a web browser and go to the address your administrator gave you. This will look something like:

```
https://192.168.1.x
```

Your browser may show a security warning the first time you visit. This is because the app uses a self-signed security certificate. Click **Advanced** and then **Proceed** (or equivalent in your browser) to continue. You will only need to do this once.

### Creating an account

1. On the login page, click **Create account**
2. Enter your name, email address, and a password
3. Click **Sign up**
4. You will be logged in automatically

### Logging in

1. Enter your email address and password
2. Click **Log in**

If you forget your password, contact your administrator.

---

## Boards

A board represents a project or area of work. Each board contains columns, and each column contains tasks.

### Creating a board

1. From the home screen, click **New board**
2. Give the board a name
3. Click **Create**

The new board will open with a default set of columns (To do, In progress, Done). You can rename or delete these.

### Switching between boards

Click the board name in the top navigation bar to see all your boards and switch between them.

### Deleting a board

1. Open the board
2. Click the **...** menu in the top right
3. Click **Delete board**
4. Confirm the deletion

> **Note:** Deleting a board permanently deletes all columns and tasks inside it. This cannot be undone.

---

## Columns

Columns represent stages in your workflow — for example: To do, In progress, In review, Done.

### Adding a column

1. On a board, scroll to the right until you see **+ Add column**
2. Click it, type a name, and press **Enter**

### Renaming a column

1. Click the column name
2. Type the new name
3. Press **Enter**

### Deleting a column

1. Click the **...** menu at the top of the column
2. Click **Delete column**
3. Confirm

> **Note:** Deleting a column deletes all tasks inside it.

### Reordering columns

Click and drag a column header left or right to reorder it.

---

## Tasks

Tasks are the individual cards within a column. A task might represent a piece of work, a bug, a feature, or anything else you want to track.

### Creating a task

1. Click **+ Add task** at the bottom of any column
2. Type the task title
3. Press **Enter** to save

### Opening a task

Click anywhere on a task card to open its detail view.

### Moving a task

Click and drag a task card to move it to a different column or a different position within the same column. The board updates in real time for everyone viewing it.

### Editing a task title

1. Open the task
2. Click the title
3. Edit it and click away to save

---

## Task details

When you open a task, you can add more information to it.

### Description

Click the description area to add or edit a longer description of the task. Useful for background information, requirements, or notes.

### Assigning a team member

1. Open the task
2. Click **Assign** (or the current assignee's name)
3. Select a team member from the list

The assigned person will receive a notification.

### Due date

1. Open the task
2. Click **Set due date**
3. Pick a date from the calendar

You will receive a notification when a due date is approaching.

### Subtasks

Subtasks are a checklist of smaller steps within a task.

1. Open the task
2. In the **Subtasks** section, click **Add subtask**
3. Type the subtask title and press **Enter**
4. Click the checkbox next to a subtask to mark it complete

### Comments

Use comments to discuss a task with your team.

1. Open the task
2. Scroll to the **Comments** section
3. Type your comment and click **Post**

### Attachments

You can attach files to a task — images, documents, spreadsheets, and so on.

1. Open the task
2. Click **Attach file**
3. Select a file from your computer
4. The file will upload and appear in the **Attachments** section

Click an attachment to download it.

---

## Real-time collaboration

The board updates live for all users who have it open. When a team member moves a task, adds a comment, or makes any change, you will see it immediately without needing to refresh the page.

A small indicator at the top of the board shows which team members are currently viewing it.

---

## Notifications

The app sends notifications for:

- Being assigned to a task
- A comment being added to a task you are assigned to
- A due date approaching (you will be notified the day before)

Notifications appear in the bell icon at the top right of the screen.

---

## Activity log

Every task has an activity log showing the full history of changes — who moved it, who changed the title, who commented, and when. This is useful for auditing and understanding how a task progressed.

To view the activity log:

1. Open a task
2. Scroll to the **Activity** section at the bottom

---

## Your account

### Changing your name or email

1. Click your name or avatar in the top right
2. Click **Profile**
3. Make your changes and click **Save**

### Changing your password

1. Click your name or avatar in the top right
2. Click **Profile**
3. Click **Change password**
4. Enter your current password and your new password
5. Click **Update password**

### Logging out

Click your name or avatar in the top right, then click **Log out**.

---

## Tips

- You can link related tasks across different boards. Open a task, scroll to **Linked tasks**, and search for the task you want to link.
- Drag tasks to the top of a column to give them higher priority visually.
- Use subtasks to break large pieces of work into smaller, trackable steps.
- The activity log is a useful record if you need to explain decisions or review what happened on a project.

---

## Getting help

If something is not working as expected, contact your administrator.
